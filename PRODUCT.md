# Product: Cliniq HIS

## Users
- **Nurses**: High-frequency users focused on patient intake, vitals, and clinical notes. Efficiency and speed are paramount.
- **Doctors**: Focus on diagnostics, orders, and review. Need clear information hierarchy.
- **Admin/Billing**: Manage records and financial claims. Accuracy over speed.

## Brand & Tone
- **Professional & Trustworthy**: Medical-grade reliability.
- **Efficient**: Minimal friction for high-stress environments (ER).
- **Modern**: Clean, high-contrast, "medical" aesthetic without being cold.
